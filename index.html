About the Hotel

A Quiet Palace on the Ajman Corniche

Corniche Palace Hotel is a full-service property in Rumaila 2, just 1.1 km from Ajman Beach. Once known as Sham Palace and Jasmine Ajman Hotel, our reimagined retreat blends traditional Arabian architecture with contemporary comfort.

Enjoy individual garden areas, an outdoor pool, free parking, and 24-hour concierge — all wrapped in genuine Emirati warmth.
